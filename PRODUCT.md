# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students, self-learners, and early-career specialists who want a structured way to learn complex topics without manually assembling courses, notes, practice tasks, and review schedules.

## Product Purpose

AI Study Agent turns a learning goal into a guided course: diagnostic questions, a knowledge graph, generated theory, practice, spaced repetition, progress tracking, and an AI mentor. Success means the visitor quickly understands that this is a serious learning system, not a generic chatbot.

## Brand Personality

Focused, exacting, composed. The brand should feel like a capable study room with an intelligent operating layer: quiet, professional, technically confident, and personal without being playful.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple gradients, oversized centered hero text, neon glow buttons, emoji-heavy badges, repetitive icon-card grids, and vague "AI tutor" marketing copy. The page should not look like a templated SaaS landing generated in one prompt.

## Design Principles

1. Show the learning system, not the hype around AI.
2. Lead with structure, progress, and trust before decoration.
3. Use restraint as a sign of confidence, not as emptiness.
4. Make every visual element carry product meaning.
5. Keep copy concrete, short, and grounded in real learner outcomes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Support keyboard navigation and reduced motion. Do not rely on color alone to communicate state. Keep Russian copy readable and avoid dense long lines on mobile.
